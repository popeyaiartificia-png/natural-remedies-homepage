# Natural Remedies Website Analysis (naturalremedy.com)

Based on an analysis of the homepage of Natural Remedies, the following details outline the components, information, and services provided.

## 1. Website Components

The website is composed of the following distinct structural and feature components:

- **Header / Navigation Menu:** Features a language selector (over 20 countries supported), Home, About Us, Products (Ruminants & Monogastrics), Our Expertise, Blogs & Insights, Careers, Sustainability, Contact Us.
- **Hero/Banner Section:** Tagline "Leading Innovations In Animal Nutrition And Healthcare."
- **Core Focus Areas Component:** Links and graphics for specific animal care areas (Monogastrics, Ruminants, Sustainability Journey).
- **R&D / Innovation Center Spotlight:** Highlights their "World-class R&D centre," Research Bulletins, Technical Case Studies, and Research Articles (e.g., Phytocee, Kolin plus). Includes downloadable PDF articles.
- **Events Component:** Display past events (e.g., 12th Kolkata International Poultry Fair) and upcoming events (e.g., International Farmers Summit 2026).
- **Company Vision Section:** Mission statement detailing their approach, "We harness nature and apply science for health and happiness sustainably."
- **Latest Blogs/Insights Widget:** Displays recent blog posts focusing on topics such as Poultry Care, Coccidiosis in Poultry, Choline Chloride's Hygroscopic Effects, etc.
- **Quick Links Banner:** Direct links to their sister verticals: Human Healthcare and Pet Care.
- **Footer Section:** Contains Quick Links, Policies (Customer Support, Privacy Policy, Disclaimer), Contact Us (Phone numbers, Facebook, LinkedIn, YouTube links).
- **Locations Block:** Lists the corporate office address alongside their manufacturing factory locations.
- **Customer Feedback Experience Widget:** A star-rating survey tool capturing visiting intent, navigation ease, and final thoughts ("Help us provide a better experience!").

## 2. Information Gathered

- **Company Profile:** Natural Remedies is a global animal health care company with over three decades of experience.
- **Core Philosophy:** They focus on innovating and developing natural solutions for veterinarians and livestock producers to facilitate profitable farming practices. They develop solutions rooted in the Indian system of medicine (Ayurveda) backed by research-based science.
- **Global Reach:** Their products and solutions have a presence in over 45 countries, highlighting a vast global logistics and distribution network.
- **Specialization (Phytogenics):** They are pioneers in phytogenics, specializing in standardized botanical powders and extracts that offer a high margin of safety and efficacy for target animal species.
- **Target Audience:** Veterinarians, livestock producers, and farmers facing modern challenges like changing feed practices and emerging diseases.
- **R&D Capability:** They have a dedicated "Natural Remedies Innovation Centre," leading robust scientific evaluations (e.g., transcriptome analysis, evaluating polyherbal formulations substituting synthetic alternatives like synthetic choline chloride).
- **Locations:**
  - **Headquarter/Office:** Electronic City, Bangalore, Karnataka, India.
  - **Factories:** Located in Krishnagiri District (Tamilnadu) and Pithampur, Dhar District (Madhya Pradesh).

## 3. Services Provided

- **Animal Health Solutions (Ruminants & Monogastrics):** Formulation and provision of natural, herbal, and phytogenic health solutions and dietary supplements targeting specific livestock types.
- **Research and Development (R&D):** Conducting scientific studies on animal nutrition and disease prevention, sharing technical case studies and research bulletins with the farming community.
- **Educational Insights:** Offering continuous updates through blogs regarding animal healthcare trends, feed safety (e.g., Non-GMO certification), and disease management (e.g., Coccidiosis).
- **Human Healthcare Solutions:** Operating an allied branch for natural human healthcare solutions.
- **Pet Care Products:** Operating an allied branch providing holistic and natural products for pets.
- **Industry Engagement & Events:** Setting up and participating in industry summits and expos (such as the International Farmers Summit) to educate stakeholders and showcase innovations.
