Our Story

Three Decades of
Animal Health Innovation

"We harness nature and apply science for health and happiness sustainably."

Natural Remedies is a global animal healthcare company with over three decades of experience. We focus on innovating and developing natural solutions for veterinarians and livestock producers to facilitate profitable farming practices.

Our solutions are deeply rooted in the Indian system of medicine — Ayurveda — backed by rigorous research-based science. We are pioneers in phytogenics, specializing in standardized botanical powders and extracts that offer a high margin of safety and efficacy for target animal species.
